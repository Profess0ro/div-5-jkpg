Torpa AIS

Placement last year:
9th place, division 5



Name and role in the team:
Erik Persson, manager

What are your goals for the season?
We do not have a stated objective for the 2024 season in particular. However, the club aims to establish itself as a team in division 5.

Any changes in the squad for this season?
Stable, occasional expected drop. Some interesting names in, but not everything is clear.

Who in the team will play a big role this season and why?
Our goalkeeper is important to the team, probably one of the best in the league (August Lindström). Our strikers need to get the goalscoring going properly.

Which team are you looking forward to play against this season and why?
We don't have a "Ydrederby", so the match against Sommen should probably be seen as the most interesting.

Which team do you think will win this year?
Hard to say. Two outsiders that I think will place high are Eksjö and Ekenässjön. Höreda will be among the top.
