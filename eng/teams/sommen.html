Sommens AIF

Placement last year:
3rd place, division 5 (qualified for division 4)



Name and role in the team:
Johan Andersson, manager(new for 2024)

What are your goals for the season?
With the very young squad we have, we have set ourselves the goal of make it through and stay on the right side of the qualifying line in the bottom of the league.

Any changes in the squad for this season?
The playing squad is very large. We have decided to run three senior teams this year. On the losing side, it is the brothers "Mo" and "Abbas" Al-Maliki who have gone to Husqvarna IF and IF Hallby. As well as last year's successful coach Alfred Palmér. On the new acquisition side, there are 10 players from Boxholms IF aged 16-21, with merits from both Boxholm's div 4 and div 6 teams in recent years..

Who in the team will play a big role this season and why?
William Fager (b.2001), as he contributes to the calmness of the game from his centre-back position, but also Noah Röjås (b.2006), who is the technical player every team needs.

Which team are you looking forward to play against this season and why?
Of course the derbies against Torpa AIS are always interesting, but also the teams we didn't meet last year.

Which team do you think will win this year?
Eksjö invested a little extra for this year, but I guess that Bäckseda will also challenge for the top.
