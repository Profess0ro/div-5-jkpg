Name and role in the team:
Emil Daag, manager

What are your goals for the season?
We haven't talked about goal setting yet within the team, usually done at the camp in April. But we absolutely must fight at the top.

Any changes in the squad for this season?
We have one who is away traveling and will miss the entire season. On the inside front, we have 2-3 players who are testing with us now. Our captain since many seasons back, has come back after years of house building and kids.

Who in the team will play a big role this season and why?
I think Axel Vadsten will play a big role for us, he grew last season and is useful in many positions on the pitch. A smart and durable player who has leadership qualities and has a good eye for the game.

Which team are you looking forward to play against this season and why?
All teams and matches have their charm at this level, I think. But Solberga and Sommen, who we lost both matches against last year, will be interesting to face.

Which team do you think will win this year?
Hard to say. I think we have a good chance to challenge without having a great grasp of the other teams.
